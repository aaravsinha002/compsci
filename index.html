Career Overview

The Purpose of Computer Science

Computer Science professionals are the architects of our digital world. They design, develop, and maintain the software systems that power everything from smartphones to space exploration. In today's technology-driven society, computer scientists solve complex problems, create innovative solutions, automate tasks, advance knowledge, and connect people and information.
